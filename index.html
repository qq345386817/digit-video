Quick SEO FAQ

What is Dig It for?

Dig It is for extracting cleaner keyframes from video so you can review covers, thumbnails, stills, and tutorial frames faster.

Does Dig It upload videos?

No, it is designed to process video locally on your device without uploading clips to a server.

Who should use it?

Creators, editors, and product teams who need useful video stills for previews, docs, and reusable visuals.

What it does

Import a clip once, then review the frames most worth keeping instead of digging through a noisy frame dump. Processing stays on your device.

Best for

Cover picks, product stills, tutorial steps, and quick content assets you want to reuse elsewhere.
