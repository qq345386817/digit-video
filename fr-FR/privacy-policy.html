Politique de confidentialité

Dig It est conçu pour traiter les vidéos localement sur votre appareil.

Local processing

Keyframe extraction runs on device. Clips are not uploaded to a server during analysis.

Photos access

Photos access is used only to choose source media and save exported results when you ask it to.

Support email

If you email support, we receive the information you choose to include in your message.
