Create a cover image from a video

A strong cover frame makes a video easier to recognize in notes, listings, tutorials, and social posts.

App StoreKeyframe guide

Use Cover mode

Cover mode is designed for fewer, stronger stills. It helps you avoid scanning the full timeline manually when you only need one or two representative images.

Look for readable composition

Choose frames where the subject is centered, text is readable, and the scene is not between two actions. For product videos, prefer frames that clearly show the item and context.

Export selected frames

Select the candidate cover images, export them as PNG files, and use them in your listing, document, presentation, or post.
