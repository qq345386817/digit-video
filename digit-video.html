DigIt Video Keyframes is Dig It

Some people search for this app as DigIt Video, DigIt keyframes, or DigIt Video Keyframes. The App Store name is Dig It: Video Keyframes.

App StoreOpen homepage

What the app does

Dig It extracts cleaner keyframes, cover stills, and step-by-step images from local videos on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Import a video, choose an extraction mode, review the frames, and export only the PNG images worth keeping.

Why local processing matters

The extraction workflow runs on your device. You do not need to upload clips to a server just to find useful still images.

Useful workflows

Use Dig It for thumbnails, product cover images, tutorial steps, documentation screenshots, notes, and social posts.
