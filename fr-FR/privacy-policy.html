Politique de confidentialité Dig It

Dig It is designed to extract and review video keyframes locally. This policy explains what data is handled by the app and website.

App Store

Overview

Dig It helps you extract and review keyframes from videos. We aim to keep the product simple, local-first, and respectful of your privacy.

Accounts

You do not need to create an account to use Dig It.

Media processing

Your selected videos and images are processed on your device unless you choose to export or share files yourself.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services to track your activity in Dig It.
