Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Dig It handles information when you use the app or visit this website.

Dig It is designed to help you extract and review keyframes from video. We aim to keep the product simple, local-first, and respectful of your privacy, including processing your videos on-device without uploading them to a server.

Privacy at a glance

  • No account is required.
  • Media processing stays on-device unless you export or share files yourself.
  • We do not use third-party analytics to track Dig It usage.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at cameoshell09@gmail.com.

Accounts

You do not need to create an account to use Dig It.

Personal information

Dig It does not require your name, email address, or other personal profile information in order to run.

We do not collect your keystrokes, private documents, or unrelated device activity.

If you install the app through Apple platforms, Apple may provide platform-level purchase or download handling, but we do not receive personal identity details from that installation process.

App data

Dig It stores app data locally on your device, such as settings and project-related files needed to support extraction results and saved snapshots.

Your media processing stays on-device, and Dig It does not upload your videos to a server unless you choose to export or share files yourself.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services to track your activity in Dig It.

Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page.

We encourage you to review this page occasionally so you stay informed about any changes.

Your acceptance of this policy

By using Dig It or visiting this website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
